Perceptron

Perceptron is an idle game where you build a neural network.

Information Collection and Use

This app automatically collects the following data:

  • Error and Crash Reports This includes the device ID, operating system version, timestamp of the error, and a full stack trace of the error. If your device does not encounter a crash or error this information is not sent. This data is used to diagnose issues that players may experience.
  • Analytics Data This includes time stamps of when certain in game milestones have been reached, device model and OS, as well as geographic info. This data is used to determine progress of players so that I can better address points in the app that are problematic.
All the information above is stored by Google via their Firebase service. It is kept confidential and is not shared except for bulk anonymized statistics (ie: 32% of players made it to X).

In addition this app uses the following third party services.

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Games

Information collected by AdMob is only used internally by google and is not shared. Information on Google Play Games is stored and managed by Google and may be visible to other players.
