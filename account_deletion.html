Account Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email to andrew [at] andrewzuo.com using the email address associated with your account.

Please use "[Account Deletion]" in the email subject line and mention the app name as well as approximately when the account was created/last used.

We will process your request and once completed you will be notified via email.

Note that once your account is deleted all associated data will also be deleted. This account deletion method is for if you no longer have access to the app. If you have access consider deleting your account from within the app.
